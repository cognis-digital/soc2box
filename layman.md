soc2box is a command-line tool that helps small companies prepare for a SOC 2 security audit without hiring a consultant or paying for expensive compliance software. You point it at your own server or laptop, tell it what security evidence you have collected (like access-review screenshots, backup logs, or scan reports), and it tracks whether each required control is current or overdue. It gives you a live readiness score and a prioritized list of gaps so you always know exactly what to fix before your next audit.
